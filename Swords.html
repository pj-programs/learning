What is a sword?

The Sword is a Tool

Swords can:

  • Thrust
  • Slash

"Club?" That's right- club. Whether it's sharp or dull, a sword is a stick of forged metal being swung at high speeds towards an opponent. I don't know about you, but I would not want to be on the other end of that steel.
